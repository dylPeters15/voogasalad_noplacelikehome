No Place Like Home - Help Screen

Creating a Sprite:

  • Click on the panel for the sprite you wish to create. (e.g. Unit, Terrain)
  • Click the plus button located in the right corner
  • Follow the wizard steps to customize your own sprite.

Adding a Sprite:

  • Click on the panel for the sprite you wish to create. (e.g. Unit, Terrain)
  • Click on the sprite of your choice 
  • Click on the grid cell that you want to add the sprite to

Move Through the Grid

  • Click and drag either the main grid or the minimap

Using the Chat Box

  • ALL - sends message to every player in game
  • TEAM - sends message to every player in your team
  • WHISPER - sends message to a player of your choice

Begin Playing Game:

  • Click View in the menu bar
  • Click Play Mode
