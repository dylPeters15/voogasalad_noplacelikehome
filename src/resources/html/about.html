No Place Like Home - About Screen

 

Welcome to No Place Like Home, a platform that lets you and your friends create and play a game of your own.  Simply create a game in our editor mode, save it, and start playing.  

Due to our flexible design, the games that you can create are limitless! From simple games like Tic Tac Toe and Checkers, to complex worldbuilding games such as Civilization, if you can think it, you can create it.

Create new units, terrains, and even abilities.  Add your own rules and end conditions.  Play on your own or in teams.  It's all up to you!
